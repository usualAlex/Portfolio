Armorcraft







PLATFORM: Android / iOS
ENGINE: Unity
GENRE: Strategy / Base Building / F2P / MMO
DEVELOPER / PUBLISHER: xFuture Technology Co.
STATUS: Canceled
MY ROLE: UI/UX Designer


A canceled game i worked on UI for 3 years.

Armorcraft was a strategy game - base building, alliance warfare, PvP and PvE. Unfortunately, the game has been canceled following beta testing, as the results and player feedback did not meet expectations.

A complex and challenging UI. I had to design a simple to use UI with a lot of information required to display on a tiny phone screen.



Style & Art Direction

Exploring different UI styles

The biggest challange for the team was finding the right direction for the game. I was designing different styles for UI early on in relation to the changes made throughout the game. A lot of work was involved in figuring out which direction to take. It started as a military theme using greens and metals and ended as a sci fi style with a dose of punk.
